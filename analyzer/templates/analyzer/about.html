The Analysis Algorithm

Our engine processes historical data points across a 3-year window to calculate the Weighted Obsolescence Index (WOI). The system evaluates three primary metrics:

Velocity

Year-over-Year (YoY) growth in job market listings.

Saturation

Current demand vs. historical peaks to identify decay.

Internal Workflow

The system utilizes a Model-View-Template (MVT) architecture. Raw market data is ingested via an ORM, processed through a statistical utility, and rendered using interactive JavaScript components.
